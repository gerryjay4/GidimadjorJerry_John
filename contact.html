Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with me

What services do you offer?

I specialize in web development services including responsive website design, frontend development, custom web applications, website optimization, and maintenance. I work with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and other modern web technologies.

What's your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. A simple website typically takes 1-2 weeks, while more complex web applications can take 4-8 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you work with clients remotely?

Yes! I work with clients worldwide through video calls, email, and project management tools. I'm experienced in remote collaboration and ensure clear communication throughout the project.

What information do you need to start a project?

I'll need details about your project goals, target audience, preferred design style, functionality requirements, timeline, and budget. Any existing branding materials or content would also be helpful.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes, I offer ongoing maintenance and support packages. This includes updates, security monitoring, performance optimization, and technical support to keep your website running smoothly.

What's your payment structure?

I typically work with a 50% upfront payment and 50% upon completion for smaller projects. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments. All payment terms are discussed and agreed upon before starting work.
